About Kiboko Garage

African Excellence in Automotive Service

Located in the Unga Ltd Area of Arusha, Kiboko Garage is a premier vehicle repair and fleet maintenance facility. We cater to individual car owners, corporate clients, and fleet operators who seek uncompromising precision, premium service, and long-term vehicle reliability.

Common Inquiries

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about our spare parts, booking process, or fleet services? Find answers below.

Do you service all vehicle brands?
Yes, our certified mechanics are fully trained to service Japanese, European, and American vehicle makes, including Toyota, Ford, Nissan, Mercedes-Benz, BMW, and commercial trucks.
How long does a computer diagnostics scan take?
A standard computer scan and ECU fault retrieval takes approximately 30 to 45 minutes. Our mechanics will then explain the diagnostic report and present repair recommendations.
Do you use genuine OEM spare parts?
Yes. We strictly utilize genuine Original Equipment Manufacturer (OEM) or high-quality certified aftermarket spares. We offer warranty on spare parts procured and fitted by our workshop.
Can I schedule my appointment online?
Yes! You can use our online Booking Portal or Hero Widget to select dates and times. You will receive an instant booking ID, and you can log in to view your service history from the client dashboard.
Do you provide fleet services for corporate clients?
Absolutely. We provide contract-based fleet maintenance programs for corporate offices, transport companies, and NGOs, complete with detailed health reports and monthly service accounts.
